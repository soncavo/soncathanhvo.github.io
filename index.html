I am an applied linguist with a Ph.D. in Applied Linguistics and Technology from Iowa State University. My research focuses on language assessment, multilingualism, and the role of intellectual humility in education. I am also passionate about integrating linguistic theory with real-world assessment practices to promote fairness and validity. In addition to my research, I have extensive experience teaching applied linguistics courses, and I enjoy mentoring students and collaborating with colleagues across disciplines.
