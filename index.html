About Me

Welcome! I am a multilingual scholar and applied linguist specializing in language assessment, multilingual education, and teacher professional development. My work spans both U.S. and international contexts, with a strong commitment to equity and inclusion in education.

Research

My research focuses on language testing validity, intellectual humility in assessment, and the role of multilingualism in education. I collaborate on projects funded by national and international organizations, integrating quantitative and qualitative methods.
